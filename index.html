Flareon, the Flame Pokémon, is a Fire-type evolution of Eevee, known for its fiery body, bushy mane, and tail, capable of reaching temperatures over 3,000°F by heating inhaled air in its flame sac, which it exhales as fire, using its fur collar to regulate its extreme body heat that is around 1,650°F

Umbreon, the Moonlight Pokémon, is a Dark-type evolution of Eevee, known for its glowing rings that appear in darkness, striking fear into foes as it waits silently to attack, often lurking at night where its large eyes pierce the dark to find prey. Its evolution is triggered by moonlight or moon energy, filling it with power, and its rings glow when it's ready to strike or leap.
