Eevee is a Normal-type Pokémon with an unstable genetic makeup that allows it to adapt and evolve into many different forms, such as Vaporeon, Jolteon, and Umbreon. Its Pokedex entries highlight its ability to mutate due to environmental factors and the radiation from evolutionary stones, making it unique among Pokémon

Tauros is the "Wild Bull Pokémon," a Normal-type creature known for its violent nature and powerful charges, which it uses to lock horns with opponents. It is a 100% male Pokémon, has a height of 4'07", and a weight of 194.9 lbs. The three Paldean forms—Combat, Blaze, and Aqua—are Normal/Fighting types.
